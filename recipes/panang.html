panang with shrimp

description

a tasty meal with panang curry with thai shrimps

Ingredients

  • coconut milk and cream
  • onion
  • shrimp
  • bean sprouts
  • fish sauce
  • panang curry paste
  • coriander
  • kaffir lime leaves
  • green beans
  • white pepper
  • peanuts
  • palm sugar
  • capsicum

steps

  1. press the peanuts into crumbs, add onions and curry paste into a bowl.
  2. add oil to pan and heat it up, then add the content in the bowl to the pan. wait until the peanuts turns golden
  3. add some coconut milk and cream to the pan
  4. let all the contents boil for a while
  5. Add the beans and capsicum and then wait
  6. add shrimps and wait ca 2 to 3 minutes
  7. add remaining things in the pan and eat!
