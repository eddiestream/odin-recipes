everyday sushi

description

everyday sushi you can do in a rush

Ingredients

  • raw salmon
  • sea weed
  • sushi rice
  • vinager
  • sesam seeds

steps

  1. press sushi rice with vinager on a sushi rolling mat so it cover ca 70
  2. take seaweed and cut it relative to the rice height
  3. cut raw salmon strips as long as the height of the rice on the mat
  4. then roll roll it
  5. roll the maki iin sesam seeds
